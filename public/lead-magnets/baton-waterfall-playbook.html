Waterfall

One cascade routes the whole seller market

Every owner flows top to bottom and lands in the first tier they match. Most route to exactly one message, and the catch-all covers the rest.

first match wins 
1. IF listed on a marketplace, broker-listed, AND listing_age < 180 days -> Tier 1 (Listed with a full-fee broker)
2. IF listed but listing_age >= 180 days OR price_reduced OR for-sale-by-owner -> Tier 2 (Stalled or FSBO)
3. IF not listed AND years_in_operation >= 15 AND (owner_tenure >= 15 OR est. age 55+) -> Tier 3 (In the succession window)
4. IF any remaining established owner-operated U.S. business in a target vertical -> Catch-all (No exit signal yet)
