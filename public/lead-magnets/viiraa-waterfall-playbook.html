Waterfall

How the waterfall works

Exclusive routing, priority order, 100% market coverage.

first match wins 
1. IF Site, LinkedIn or Instagram mentions CGM, or sells a glucose program -> Tier 1 - CGM-active (They already believe in the modality but are cobbling it together by hand, with no product around it.)
2. IF Positions on weight, blood sugar, pre-diabetes, longevity or GLP-1, but no CGM detected -> Tier 2 - Metabolic-adjacent (Adjacent and ready, leaving the highest-conviction modality and its revenue on the table.)
3. IF None of the above (any remaining health or wellness practitioner) -> Tier 3 - Catch-all (Every remaining practitioner still needs a program clients can visibly see working.)

Launch

Launch plan and thresholds

Phase-roll from highest conviction to broadest.

launch first
01

Phase 1: Launch Tier 1 (CGM-active) first. Smallest market, highest conviction, fastest proof. The CGM signal is unambiguous and the pain is sharp.

02

Phase 2: Add Tier 2 (Metabolic-adjacent) once Tier 1 shows at least 1 positive reply per 300. Biggest adjacent-and-ready pool.

03

Phase 3: Open the Catch-all last, once the sharper tiers have produced validated copy patterns to borrow from.

04

Parallel: Run the GLP-1 clinic side campaign from week one. Timely and high-intent enough to justify its own lane outside the waterfall.

Scale / cut rules

1 positive reply / 150 contacts Perfect. Scale this tier hard.
1 / 300 Good. Let it run.
1 / 450-500 Borderline. Test a copy variant, vary the lead magnet or first-line research angle.
Worse than 1 / 500, or reply rate under 1% after 1,500+ sends Cut and rework the tier.
