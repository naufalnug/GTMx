Launch

Launch narrow, then scale what replies

Start with the deflection-to-conversion tier, prove reply density, then roll the winning language into the wider waterfall.

launch first
01

Launch Tier 1 (Deflection to Conversion) first. Smallest TAM, hottest intent, cleanest before/after revenue story against a named tool.

02

Add Tier 2 (Product-Discovery Friction) one week later. The same directory pull already surfaces catalog size.

03

Layer Tier 3 (24/7 Multilingual), then turn on the Catch-All once upstream copy is validated.

04

Hold everything but the lead magnet and notice-line angle constant. Two variants per tier max.

05

Do not launch any tier under 1,500 contacts, there isn't enough statistical signal to judge it.

Scale / cut rules

1 positive reply / 150 contacts Perfect. Scale the tier hard.
1 / 300 Good. Let it run.
1 / 450-500 Borderline. Test a copy variant (vary the lead magnet or notice-line angle first).
Worse than 1 / 500 or <1% after 1,500 sends Cut the tier and rework trigger or offer.
