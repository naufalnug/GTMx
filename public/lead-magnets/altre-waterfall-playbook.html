Waterfall

How the waterfall works

Exclusive routing, priority order, 100% market coverage.

first match wins 
1. IF Raised Series A+ within the last 6 months -> Tier 1 - Freshly Funded (Fresh capital plus a mandate to scale means an office move is imminent.)
2. IF In a coworking operator AND hiring 10+ roles -> Tier 2 - Coworking Overflow (Per-seat flex economics break past ~50 heads.)
3. IF 15+ open roles in a metro OR 25%+ headcount growth -> Tier 3 - Hiring Seat Crunch (Team is outgrowing the current floorplate.)
4. IF None of the above -> Tier 4 - Catch-all (Every remaining enterprise still faces an under-managed real estate cost centre.)

Launch

Launch plan and thresholds

Phase-roll from highest intent to broadest.

launch first
01

Phase 1: Launch Tier 1 (Freshly Funded) first. Smallest market, highest intent, fastest proof. Funding lists refresh weekly so the tier self-replenishes.

02

Phase 2: Add Tier 2 (Coworking Overflow) once Tier 1 shows at least 1 positive reply per 300. The breakeven analysis is a strong, concrete magnet.

03

Phase 3: Roll Tier 3 (Hiring Seat Crunch), then open the Catch-all last, once sending infrastructure is fully warmed.

04

Parallel: Run the GCC / India-entry press side-campaign from week one. Low volume, very high intent, and sits outside the waterfall.

Scale / cut rules

1 positive reply / 150 contacts Perfect. Scale this tier hard.
1 / 300 Good. Let it run.
1 / 450-500 Borderline. Test a copy variant, vary the magnet or first-line research angle.
Worse than 1 / 500, or reply rate under 1% after 1,500+ sends Cut and rework the tier.
