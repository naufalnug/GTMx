Waterfall

The waterfall approach

One routed cascade beats ten parallel campaigns. Build once, add leads forever.

first match wins 
1. IF sells_physical_product is false -> Drop from market (Not the ICP; Hitfactor sells to businesses moving physical product on YouTube.)
2. IF hiring_video_role is true -> Tier 1 - Investing in video headcount (A live video-role job post is the strongest possible proof of committed spend.)
3. IF channel found AND 8+ uploads in 90 days AND views flat or declining -> Tier 2 - Committed but plateauing (Consistent output with stalling returns implies they cannot predict what will land.)
4. IF channel found AND views at or above niche median AND no product link in videos -> Tier 3 - Views without sales (Attention without a purchase path means YouTube is a cost, not a revenue line.)
5. IF anything else with a channel -> Tier 4 - Catch-all (Guarantees 100% coverage. Any physical-product channel is a candidate.)

1 positive reply per 150 contacts Perfect. Scale that tier.
1 per 300 Good. Let it run.
