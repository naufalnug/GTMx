Waterfall

One cascade routes every advertiser

Active OOH intent, then footprint size, then vertical fit create exclusive tiers, from brands already buying out-of-home down to a catch-all covering every target-vertical advertiser.

first match wins 
1. IF ooh_field_marketing_signal in [open_role, existing_title] -> Tier 1 (Active OOH / field-marketing intent)
2. IF us_location_count >= 100 -> Tier 2 (Large multi-location footprint)
3. IF us_location_count between 10 and 99 -> Tier 3 (Regional / franchise footprint)
4. IF any remaining target-vertical advertiser with a marketing DM -> Catch-all (Verified OOH at scale)

Scale / cut rules

1 positive reply / 150 contacts Perfect. Scale the tier.
1 / 300 Good. Let it run and phase the next tier.
1 / 450-500 Borderline. Rotate the lead magnet or first-line angle.
Worse than 1 / 500 or <1% after 1,500 sends Cut the tier and rework the trigger or offer.
