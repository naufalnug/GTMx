The client: Sendr.ai

Sendr.ai is the most personal engagement platform in sales: an all-in-one outbound CRM that replaces the messy outbound stack (lead finder, enrichment, personalised pages, dynamic video, campaigns, tracking) with one clean workflow. GDPR and ISO 27001 certified, 500M+ refreshed B2B profiles, 5M+ personalised pages generated, 12K+ meetings booked across its customer base.

The product was exceptional. The problem: in late 2025 nobody could find it on Google, and ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude and Perplexity had no idea it existed. Sendr was competing in a category owned by 8-figure incumbents like ZoomInfo, with none of their budget. The brief to RankedTag was one sentence: become the first name that comes up when someone asks about modern outbound, on Google and inside every major LLM.

Why traditional SEO alone would have failed

Traditional SEO alone is a 2024 strategy. In 2026, buyers are split: half search Google, half ask ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude or Perplexity. A modern GTM tool needs to be the answer on both surfaces.

RankedTag's Inbound Engine covers the whole stack: senior strategists pick the fights, Claude compresses the research at scale, and editors keep the craft. Nothing ships unread, nothing ships on autopilot.

§ THE STRATEGY

One engine, four disciplines.

Classic SEO for Google, AI SEO for everything else.

01 / TECHNICAL FOUNDATION

Technical SEO & schema

Before chasing traffic we fixed the plumbing: full Core Web Vitals audit, internal link architecture rebuild, JSON-LD structured data (SoftwareApplication, Organization, FAQPage), llms.txt, sitemap cleanup and crawl budget optimisation. Google re-crawled the entire site in under 72 hours.

Core Web Vitals · Schema.org · llms.txt · the 52-check stack

02 / CONTENT

Category-defining content

Pillar pages around the buying-intent clusters Sendr's ICP actually searches: "best GTM tool", "outbound CRM", "AI sales platform", "personalised video at scale", "lead enrichment alternatives". Each pillar spawned 6 to 10 supporting articles with tight internal linking. Depth and originality over volume: the kind of pages LLMs want to cite.

Topic clusters · pillar pages · comparison SEO · use-case pages

03 / AI SEO · GEO

Generative Engine Optimisation

This is what set the campaign apart. We optimised Sendr's content for how large language models retrieve and cite, not just how Google indexes: clean semantic chunking, direct answer formats, comparison tables, statistic-first paragraphs, and authoritative mentions across Reddit, G2, Capterra and Product Hunt. LLMs now reliably surface Sendr for outbound CRM and personalised-video queries.

GEO · LLM optimisation · citation building · answer engineering

04 / AEO + AUTHORITY

Answer engines & digital PR

Answer Engine Optimisation to win AI Overviews, featured snippets and PAA boxes, paired with strategic placements across GTM publications, podcast features and a quiet backlink program from genuinely relevant domains. No link farms, no PBNs: every link earned. Topical authority around "outbound" and "sales engagement" became dominant.

AEO · digital PR · founder-led content · topical authority

The stack behind it: senior human strategy, Claude for deep research and GEO citation mapping, N8N workflows for velocity, measured with Ahrefs, SEMrush, Surfer, Screaming Frog, GSC, GA4, Profound and Otterly. AI compresses the grunt work; humans handle the craft.
